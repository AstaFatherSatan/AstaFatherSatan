  • The Moon is of the element of water
  • The Moon rules the 3rd eye and 6th chakra

Jasmine, Poppy, Myrtle, Camphor, Sandalwood, Night Blooming Cereus, Opium.
Incense of the Moon is meditative and hypnotic.
