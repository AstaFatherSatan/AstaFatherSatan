The Invocation of Enki by James Page


From the royal blue flame within the mind of Enki
Boldly illuminating the minds of all
Banishing the place where true evil dwells
We bring forth light on earth!

It was Enki's love that designed us as gods
And his love still beats within our chests
For we are those within
We call forth Enki's light on earth!

From ancient time to future place
We align our will with father Enki's will
To serve the mission all Demons know and serve
We call forth Enki's light on earth!

There is a sacred center
And from it Enki called us forth
And from it comes the plan for earth
We call forth Enki's light to earth!

There is a way, The Left Hand Path
This Path will seal the place where yahwe dwells
And destroy the herd on earth
We bring forth Enki's light to earth!

Hail Enki!
Hail Satan!
