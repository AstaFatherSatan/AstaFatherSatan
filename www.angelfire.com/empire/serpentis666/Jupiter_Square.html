The mantra for Jupiter is for 16 days in a row. It is important never to skip any days, as this will cancel out the entire work.

Ideally, each day, the mantra chosen for Jupiter should be recited during the hours of Jupiter for that day. This is ideal, but regardless never skip any days, regardless of the hour.
