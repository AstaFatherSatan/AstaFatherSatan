 The Swastika 

 

The Swastika is significant in all Satanic/Pagan cultures. It is a symbol of the Sun and Cosmic order. Our Gods use this symbol as well. It is recognized all over. For all of you who do not know, all races used this symbol. It is not a symbol of hate or racism or "evil Nazis, etc"

The symbol is found in different regions in Africa on clothing, walls, pottery and even scarring the symbol on their skin decoratively.
